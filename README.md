Stream music from your browser to your whole team—no app, no account, no setup for listeners.

> **Works best for teams on the same Wi‑Fi network.**

## Get started (under 2 minutes)

### 1) Install the CLI

```bash
npm install -g localvibe
```

Start your station:

```bash
localvibe
```

After you run it, LocalVibe will print a share link like:

- `http://192.168.1.x:8012`

### 2) Download and load the broadcaster extension

1. **Download the extension zip**: https://github.com/JahidDev24/localvibe/blob/main/localvibe-extension.zip
2. Unzip it to a folder on your computer.
3. Open your browser and go to:
   - `chrome://extensions`
   (Chrome / Brave)
4. Turn on **Developer mode** (top right).
5. Click **Load unpacked** and select the unzipped folder.

### 3) Go live & share

1. Play music in any browser tab (Spotify Web, YouTube, etc.)
2. Open the LocalVibe extension and click **Start Broadcast**.
3. Copy the link from the extension and send it to your team.
4. Your listeners open the link and press play — that’s it. 📻

## Why LocalVibe

- **No app needed for listeners**: they just open a link (phones, laptops, tablets, smart TVs—no install).
- **Broadcast anything**: any playable browser tab (YouTube, Spotify Web, Apple Music, podcasts, movies).
- **Silent DJ mode**: mute your own speakers while broadcasting (listen from your phone).
- **Your station, your brand**: station name / DJ name / tagline.
- **See who’s listening**: live listener count in the player and extension.
- **Smooth playback**: built on the same HLS tech used in streaming platforms.

## Privacy

- **Only you can broadcast**: a fresh session key is generated every time you start.
- **Listeners can only listen**: the player is read-only; there’s no path from their browser into your computer.
- **Gone when you stop**: closing the broadcast removes the stream instantly.
- **Fully offline after install**: no cloud accounts/telemetry for playback; your audio stays on your network.
